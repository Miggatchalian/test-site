Professor Snape Trivia !

Do you know the difference between an
Animagus and a Warewolf ?

An Animagus ( Animagi in singular ) was a witch or wizard who could transform themselves into an animal and back again at their own will. It was a skill that could be learned, unlike the hereditary ability of a Metamorphmagus. Information on Animagi was taught to Hogwarts students during their third-year Transfiguration class. An Animagus Registry existed to keep track of those that had learned to transform in this way.

A Warewolf also known as a lycanthrope, was a creature which normally resembled a human being but, upon the complete rising of the full moon, became an uncontrollable, fearsome and deadly wolf. This condition was caused by infection with lycanthropy, also known as werewolfry. Werewolves were almost indistinguishable in appearance from the true wolf. The real difference was in behaviour.

CHARACTERS

Harry James Potter

'The Boy Who Lived'

Harry Potter is the only known survivor of the Killing Curse, which was attempted by Voldemort when Harry was just a baby, after both his parents were murdered.

Daniel Radcliffe

Main Character

Harry James Potter is the titular character of the Harry Potter series of novels by J. K. Rowling. The plot of the seven-book series chronicles seven years in the life of the orphan Harry, who, on his eleventh birthday, learns he is a wizard. He attends Hogwarts, a school of magic, where he receives guidance from the headmaster Albus Dumbledore and becomes friends with Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. Harry learns that during his infancy, the Dark wizard Lord Voldemort murdered his parents but was unable to kill him as well. The plot of the series revolves around Harry's struggle to adapt to the wizarding world and defeat Voldemort. Harry is regarded as a fictional icon and has been described by many critics, readers, and audiences as one of the greatest literary and film characters of all time. He is portrayed by Daniel Radcliffe in all eight Harry Potter films. Harry also appears in the play Harry Potter and the Cursed Child, which has been produced on stage since 2016. Potter is probably one of the bravest characters to ever exist in the history of children's literature. Despite being brought up in a non-magic family for a major period of his early life, Harry went on to defeat one of the strongest dark wizards to ever have lived.

Cedric Diggory

A dedicated and passionate Captain of the Gryffindor Quidditch team. A skilled Keeper, he pushes his team to excel, often prioritizing Quidditch above all else. His leadership and determination inspire younger players like Harry Potter. Though intense, his love for the sport and commitment make him a respected figure at Hogwarts.

Hermione Granger

A brilliant and hardworking student, known for her intelligence, dedication, and resourcefulness. Excels in magic, solving problems others cannot. Fiercely loyal to her friends, she stands by them in every challenge. As a Muggle-born, she fights against prejudice in the wizarding world with courage and determination, proving her strength.

Ron Weasley

A loyal and humorous friend who often provides comic relief but also deep courage. Coming from a large, loving family, he struggles with inadequacy but proves himself repeatedly. He fears spiders, loves wizard chess, and supports Harry through every trial. Despite moments of doubt, his loyalty and bravery shine when it matters most. His unwavering friendship makes him an essential part of the trio’s journey.

Draco Malfoy

A proud and cunning student from Slytherin, often seen as arrogant and prejudiced, he struggles with his family's expectations. Though he antagonizes Harry and his friends, he later faces moral dilemmas that test his character. Behind his bravado lies insecurity, and his journey reveals the complexity of growing up under pressure and fear.

Oliver Wood

A talented and honorable Hufflepuff student known for kindness, bravery, and integrity. As Seeker and Captain of the Hufflepuff Quidditch team, he leads with skill and sportsmanship. He gains widespread respect at Hogwarts and competes in the Triwizard Tournament, where he proves his courage. His legacy lives on as a symbol of loyalty and heroism, inspiring witches and wizards to stand against darkness.

Dobby

A devoted and courageous house-elf who initially serves the Malfoy family. Freed by Harry Potter, he dedicates his life to helping those in need. Though overly enthusiastic, his loyalty and bravery are unmatched. Aid Harry and his friends. His selfless sacrifice to save them makes him one of the most beloved characters in the series. Performed wandless magic stronger than many wizards

Buckbeak

A majestic and proud Hippogriff introduced in Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban. Highly intelligent and fiercely loyal, he only respects those who show him proper courtesy. He forms a bond with Harry and later helps in Sirius Black’s escape. Wrongly sentenced to execution, he is rescued and eventually lives under Hagrid’s care, remaining a symbol of freedom and dignity.

Rubeus Hagrid

A half-giant with a heart as big as his towering frame. As Keeper of Keys and Grounds at Hogwarts, he loves magical creatures and fiercely protects his friends. Often misunderstood due to his size, he remains gentle and kind. His unwavering faith in Dumbledore and deep affection for Harry make him one of the most beloved figures at Hogwarts.

Albus Dumbledore

Wise and powerful Headmaster of Hogwarts. Known for deep understanding of magic, compassion, and cryptic advice. A former duel partner of Grindelwald, Dumbledore harbors regrets yet dedicates his life to guiding Harry against Voldemort. His wisdom and sacrifice shape the wizarding world, leaving a legacy.

Sirius Black

A fiercely loyal and rebellious wizard, Sirius Black is Harry Potter’s godfather and a key member of the Order of the Phoenix. Wrongly imprisoned in Azkaban, he emerges determined to fight Voldemort. Brave, reckless, and protective, he treats Harry like family, offering guidance and support. Despite his troubled past, he remains devoted to justice and freedom. His tragic death leaves an irreplaceable void.

Bellatrix Lestrange

A fanatical and sadistic Death Eater, Bellatrix Lestrange is one of Voldemort’s most devoted followers. Skilled in dark magic, she relishes chaos and destruction. Unwavering in loyalty, she torments enemies, especially the Order of the Phoenix. Her cruelty knows no bounds, making her one of the most feared witches in history, spreading fear.

Severous Snape

A complex and mysterious character, known for his cold demeanor and mastery of Potions. He appears to despise Harry but secretly protects him due to his undying love for Lily Potter. A double agent for both Voldemort and Dumbledore, Snape’s true loyalties remain a secret until his tragic and heroic sacrifice, revealing his devotion.
